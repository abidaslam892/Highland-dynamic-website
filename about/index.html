About Highland

Two decades of creating mountain sanctuaries

Our Story

Founded over 20 years ago, Highland began with a simple vision: to help people reconnect with nature by creating extraordinary mountain sanctuaries. What started as a small team with a passion for alpine environments has grown into the region's premier mountain landscape design, construction, and maintenance company.

Today, we're proud to serve clients throughout the mountain communities, bringing the same dedication to excellence that defined our early years.
