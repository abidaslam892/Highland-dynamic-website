Evergreen Meadows

Converting a sloped, unusable property into functional terraced gardens. Demonstrates our expertise in grading and drainage solutions.

Project Overview

Converting a sloped, unusable property into functional terraced gardens. Demonstrates our expertise in grading and drainage solutions. This comprehensive project showcases Highland's expertise in creating extraordinary mountain sanctuaries that blend seamlessly with the natural environment.

Working closely with the property owners, our team developed a master plan that addressed unique site challenges while maximizing views and outdoor living potential. Every element was carefully selected to thrive in high-altitude conditions.

Project Details

Project Type: Residential Landscape
Services: Grading, Terracing, Problem Solving
Timeline: 6-8 months
Location: Mountain Region

Key Features

Native Plantings

Carefully selected alpine species that thrive at high altitude and require minimal maintenance.

Custom Hardscaping

Natural stone pathways and terraces that complement the mountain landscape.

Outdoor Living

Functional spaces designed for year-round enjoyment of the mountain environment.
