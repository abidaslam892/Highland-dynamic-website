Mountain Design & Architecture

Creating a Plan and a Vision

Transform Your Property into an Enchanting Mountain Oasis

Our team of mountain design experts has been crafting breathtaking alpine retreats for over two decades. We believe that meticulous planning is the key to unlocking the full potential of your property, transforming it into an oasis that will inspire and captivate you for years to come.

We transform both residential and commercial mountain properties, creating something truly unique with each project. Whether it's a private alpine sanctuary or a mountain resort experience, we bring your vision to life.

Tailored to Your Vision

We take the time to understand the unique characteristics of your site, from its topography and elevation to its climate and existing natural elements. This allows us to create a design that seamlessly integrates with its mountainous surroundings, enhancing the natural beauty of the area while also meeting your specific functional requirements.

Every mountain property tells a different story, and we're here to help you write yours.

Picture Yourself in Your Dream Mountain Retreat

Imagine hosting unforgettable gatherings on your mountain terrace, or basking in the tranquility of a private alpine garden surrounded by nature's beauty. Our team of passionate mountain designers will help you bring your vision to life, transforming your outdoor space into a place where you can relax, reconnect, and cherish every moment.

Beyond Aesthetics: Evoking Emotion

We don't just create mountain sanctuaries that look stunning; we strive to evoke a desired atmosphere and emotion, ensuring that your alpine oasis reflects your personality and becomes a sanctuary where you truly connect with nature.

Our Design Process

After a complimentary initial meeting, engaging our mountain design services begins with a design retainer that is based on the total scope of the project. Standard retainers include a plan view design, 3D rendering, consultation meeting, and a round of edits/redesign, second meeting, and an installation contract. Design timelines can range from one month to several.
