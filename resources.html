Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best time to start a mountain landscape project?

Spring and early summer are ideal for construction projects, giving new plants a full season to establish before winter. However, design work can begin anytime, and we recommend starting the planning process 3-6 months before desired construction.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary significantly based on scope. Small projects may take 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive estate transformations can span 2-4 months. We provide detailed timelines during the design phase.

Do you offer maintenance services for properties you didn't design?

Absolutely! Our maintenance programs are available for any mountain property, regardless of who completed the original installation. We'd be happy to assess your property and recommend an appropriate maintenance plan.

What makes mountain landscaping different from standard landscaping?

Mountain properties face unique challenges: shorter growing seasons, extreme temperature swings, intense UV exposure, rocky soils, and wildlife interactions. Success requires specialized knowledge of alpine plants, proper drainage solutions, and construction techniques adapted to mountain terrain.

How do I know which service tier is right for me?

We offer complimentary consultations to assess your property, understand your goals, and recommend the appropriate service level. Whether you need Essentials, Standard, or Premium services, we'll ensure it aligns with your needs and budget.

Have More Questions?

Our team is always happy to help. Whether you're planning a major project or just need advice on maintaining your existing landscape, we're here to share our expertise.
